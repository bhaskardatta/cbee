Risk zones — what could blow Week 3

  • OOM on Redmi 12 from too many mounted videos. Mitigation: mount window MUST work; profile by Day 12.
  • iOS audio session collision after Reels visit, breaking subsequent camera record. Mitigation: G-3.
  • Supabase egress spike if testers run reels in a loop. Mitigation: 60s cap + thumbnail preview.
  • TanStack Query cache stale-data with new columns. Mitigation: bump buster to v2 in App.tsx.

Mitigation already locked in

  • Three-slide mount window — implemented Day 12.
  • JS tear-down of all videos before opening camera — implemented Day 6.
  • Hard 60s cap on video duration both at camera and at gallery picker.
  • Cache buster bumped in Day 1 PR.
